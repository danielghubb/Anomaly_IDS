# Flightless Bird Facts

**Cassowary (Attacker - 10.0.0.10)**
- Casuarius casuarius
- Extremely dangerous. Can disembowel with claws.
- Lives in Australia/New Guinea rainforests
- Helmet-like casque on head

**Dodo (Victim 1 - 10.0.0.101)**
- Raphus cucullatus
- Extinct 1692
- 23kg, turkey-sized
- Could outrun humans but curious and easy to catch
- Tasted bitter except during fattening season

**Takahe (Victim 2 - 10.0.0.102)**
- Porphyrio hochstetteri
- ~550 alive (thought extinct until 1948)
- New Zealand alpine grasslands
- Lays 1-2 eggs per year

**Kiwi (Victim 3 - 10.0.0.103)**
- Apteryx mantelli
- Lays egg that's 20% of body weight
- Nostrils at tip of beak for underground foraging
- New Zealand's national symbol


**Moa (Monitor)**
- Dinornis robustus
- Extinct ~1400 CE
- 3.6m tall, 230kg (largest bird ever)
- No wings at all
- Hunted to extinction by Maori
